I am a graduate student in the computer science department at UCLA, currently pursuing Masters. My research interests include Deep learning and its applications in computer vision.

Currently, I am working on adversarial attacks on image classifiers in a black box setting. I am working with Prof. Cho-Jui Hsieh on optimizing the number of queries required to attack a model when only the class labels of the input image are accesible from the model.

Some of the projects I worked on can be found here.

Here I try to summarize any interesting research papers that I come across. The field of deep learning is growing very rapidly and there is a lot to learn. So I try to write about some of the papers that I find interesting.

Publications
